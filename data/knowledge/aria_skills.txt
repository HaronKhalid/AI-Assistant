# ARIA Skills Reference
ARIA can do the following:
- Set timers: "set a timer for 10 minutes"
- Weather: "what's the weather today"
- Volume: "volume up", "volume down", "set volume to 50"
- Open apps: "open firefox", "open terminal"
- Screenshot: "take a screenshot"
- Web search: "search for latest news"
- Notes: "save a note", "read my notes"
- Memory: "remember that my name is X", "what do you know about me"
- Clear history: "clear conversation history"
